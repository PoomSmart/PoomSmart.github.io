Compatibility

  • Compatible with iOS 7.0 +

Description

  • Make your CoreAnimation apps use the highest available FPS (same as your device's refresh rate). Head to Settings > CAHighFPS and enable the tweak for each of your apps. Tested on these apps:

  • Where's My Water 2
  • Plants vs Zombies (HD)
  • Plants vs Zombies 2
  • Bejeweled Blitz
  • Bejeweled Classic (HD)
